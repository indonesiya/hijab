Clean lines are a key feature of modern modest fashion. This style focuses on simplicity, structure, and minimal detailing. For hijab-wearing women, clean-line outfits create a polished and contemporary look. They are easy to style and suitable for various occasions. This approach combines elegance with practicality.

Structured pieces such as straight-cut dresses and tailored tops are essential in this style. These items create a neat and refined silhouette. Pairing them with simple hijab styles enhances the overall look. Neutral colors often dominate clean-line fashion. This creates a cohesive aesthetic.

Fabric choice plays an important role in maintaining structure. Materials that hold their shape help preserve clean lines طوال اليوم. Avoid overly soft fabrics that may lose form. Proper selection improves the final appearance. Balance is key in achieving this style.
