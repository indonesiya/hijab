Time management is a crucial skill for hijabi women balancing multiple responsibilities. Managing time effectively allows for better productivity and reduced stress.

Setting clear priorities helps you focus on what truly matters. Not all tasks require the same level of attention.

Creating a schedule can improve organization and ensure that tasks are completed on time. Planning ahead is key.

Avoiding distractions, especially digital ones, can significantly improve focus and efficiency.
