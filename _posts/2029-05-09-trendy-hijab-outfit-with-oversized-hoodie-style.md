Oversized hoodies bring a trendy and relaxed vibe to hijab fashion. This style is popular among younger audiences. Loose fits provide comfort and flexibility. Neutral tones create a modern look. Street style reflects individuality.

Balancing oversized pieces is important. Pairing with fitted bottoms creates structure. Avoid overly baggy combinations. A balanced outfit feels more refined. Comfort remains key.

Accessories should match the casual theme. Sneakers and simple bags work best. Avoid excessive styling. Keep the look cohesive. Small details enhance the outfit.
