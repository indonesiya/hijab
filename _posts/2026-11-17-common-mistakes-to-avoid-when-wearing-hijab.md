Wearing a hijab is a beautiful expression of modesty, but there are some common mistakes that many beginners make. Being aware of these mistakes can help you improve both your comfort and overall appearance.

One common mistake is choosing the wrong fabric, especially in hot weather. Heavy materials can feel uncomfortable and may cause excessive sweating throughout the day.

Another issue is wrapping the hijab too tightly, which can lead to discomfort and even headaches. A slightly loose style often looks more natural and feels more comfortable.

Ignoring color coordination can also affect your overall look. Wearing clashing colors may distract from your outfit rather than enhance it.
