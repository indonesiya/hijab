Some common hijab fashion mistakes can make an outfit look less neat and unbalanced. Using fabrics that are too slippery often causes hijab styles to shift throughout the day.

Over-layering can make the outfit look bulky and reduce overall elegance. Poor color matching between hijab and outfit may create visual imbalance.
