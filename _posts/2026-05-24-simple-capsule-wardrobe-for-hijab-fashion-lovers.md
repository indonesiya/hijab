A capsule wardrobe helps hijab fashion lovers simplify their clothing choices while still looking stylish every day. It consists of essential clothing pieces that can be easily mixed and matched to create multiple outfits.

Neutral colors and basic designs are the foundation of a capsule wardrobe because they are timeless and flexible. This approach reduces decision fatigue and saves time when choosing daily outfits.
