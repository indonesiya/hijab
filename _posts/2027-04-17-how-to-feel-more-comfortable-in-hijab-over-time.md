Feeling comfortable in hijab is not always instant. For many women, it takes time to adjust physically and emotionally.

At first, you may feel unfamiliar with the change in appearance and routine. This is a normal part of adaptation.

As time passes, your confidence grows and the hijab becomes a natural part of your identity.

Small adjustments in style, fabric, and routine can also improve comfort significantly.
