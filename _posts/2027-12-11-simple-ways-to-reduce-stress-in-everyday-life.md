Stress is a common part of modern life, but managing it effectively is essential for maintaining well-being. For hijabi women, simple daily practices can make a significant difference in reducing stress levels.

One effective method is organizing your tasks. Having a clear plan helps reduce uncertainty and makes daily responsibilities feel more manageable.

Taking short breaks throughout the day can also help relieve stress. These moments allow your mind to rest and recover from continuous activity.

Engaging in relaxing activities such as reading or light exercise can further support stress reduction. These activities help shift your focus and improve your mood.
