It’s easy to compare your journey with others, especially in the age of social media. Seeing perfect images and stories can sometimes make you feel like you’re not doing enough.

However, every journey is different. What works for someone else may not be suitable for you, and that’s completely okay.

Comparison often leads to unnecessary pressure and self-doubt. Instead of feeling inspired, you may start feeling discouraged.

Focusing on your own progress allows you to appreciate how far you’ve come. Even small steps are worth celebrating.
