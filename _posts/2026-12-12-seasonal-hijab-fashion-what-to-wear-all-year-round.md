Dressing according to the season is important for maintaining comfort and style. Hijabi women can adapt their outfits and hijab choices to suit different weather conditions throughout the year.

During hot seasons, lightweight fabrics and loose styles are ideal for staying cool. Choosing breathable materials helps reduce discomfort during high temperatures.

In cooler weather, layering becomes essential. Adding jackets, coats, or thicker hijabs can keep you warm while still looking fashionable.

Seasonal colors can also enhance your outfit. Bright tones work well in summer, while darker shades are often preferred during colder months.
