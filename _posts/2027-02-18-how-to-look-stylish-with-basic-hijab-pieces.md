Basic wardrobe pieces are often underestimated, but they are actually the foundation of a stylish look. For hijabi women, mastering the use of simple items can create endless outfit possibilities.

Plain hijabs in neutral tones can be paired with various outfits, making them highly versatile.

Mixing textures and layers can add interest to otherwise simple combinations.

Keeping your outfit balanced ensures that it does not look too plain or too busy.
