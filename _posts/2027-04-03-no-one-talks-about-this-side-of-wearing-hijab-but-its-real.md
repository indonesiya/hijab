While many discussions about hijab focus on beauty and confidence, there is also a side that people rarely talk about. This includes the emotional and mental adjustments that come with the journey.

At times, you may feel misunderstood or judged, especially in environments where hijab is not common. These moments can be challenging but also help build inner strength.

There are also days when motivation feels low, and doubts start to appear. This is a normal part of any personal journey and should not be seen as failure.

Learning to accept these ups and downs helps you grow stronger and more resilient. Over time, you develop a deeper understanding of yourself.
