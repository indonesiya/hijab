Proper care is essential to maintain the quality and longevity of hijabs. Regular washing helps remove dirt and oils that accumulate over time. Using gentle detergents prevents damage to delicate fabrics. Hand washing is often recommended for materials like chiffon and silk. This ensures that the fabric remains soft and durable.

Drying techniques also play a crucial role in hijab care. Air drying is preferable to maintain the shape and texture of the fabric. Avoiding direct sunlight helps prevent color fading. Folding hijabs neatly after drying keeps them organized. This makes them easier to access and use daily.

Storage is another important aspect of maintaining hijab quality. Keeping them in a clean and dry place prevents damage. Using organizers or hangers can help maintain their shape. Separating different fabrics ensures proper care for each type. This extends the lifespan of the hijabs.
