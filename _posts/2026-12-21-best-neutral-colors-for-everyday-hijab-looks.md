Neutral colors are a staple in modest fashion because of their versatility and timeless appeal. Hijabi women can rely on these colors to create effortless and stylish outfits for everyday wear.

Shades such as black, white, beige, and grey are easy to combine with various clothing pieces. These colors provide a solid foundation for any wardrobe.

Neutral tones also allow you to experiment with textures and layering without overwhelming your overall look. Balance is key in styling.

Adding one statement piece in a slightly different shade can create interest while still maintaining a cohesive appearance. Small details make a difference.
