Hijab fashion changes not only with trends but also with seasons, requiring women to adjust their outfits according to weather conditions while staying stylish and modest. In hot seasons, breathable fabrics like cotton and linen are preferred to keep the body cool while maintaining comfort during daily activities.

During colder months, layering becomes important, with long coats, knitwear, and thicker hijabs helping to maintain warmth without sacrificing style. Seasonal colors also influence hijab fashion choices, with bright tones for summer and deeper earthy tones for autumn and winter looks.
