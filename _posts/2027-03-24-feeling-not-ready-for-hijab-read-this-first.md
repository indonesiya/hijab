Many women delay wearing the hijab because they feel they are “not ready yet.” This feeling is very common and often comes from fear of change or self-doubt. However, waiting for the perfect moment may only hold you back longer.

The truth is, no one ever feels 100% ready at the beginning. Every journey starts with uncertainty, and that is completely normal. What matters is the willingness to take the first step.

Instead of focusing on perfection, try focusing on progress. Small steps can lead to meaningful changes over time. You don’t have to change everything at once.

It’s also important to remind yourself that this journey is personal. Everyone has their own pace, and comparing yourself to others will only create unnecessary pressure.
