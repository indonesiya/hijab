Layering is one of the most important techniques in modest fashion for hijabi women. It allows for creativity while maintaining coverage and comfort.

Combining different textures and lengths can create visually interesting outfits. For example, pairing a long cardigan with a dress adds depth and elegance.

Choosing lightweight fabrics ensures that layering remains comfortable throughout the day, especially in warmer climates.

Color coordination is also essential when layering. Matching tones or creating contrast can enhance the overall look.
