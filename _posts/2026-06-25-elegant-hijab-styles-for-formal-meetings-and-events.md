Formal meetings require hijab styles that look polished, professional, and elegant without being too flashy. Structured outfits like blazers, long skirts, and tailored pants help create a strong and confident appearance.

Neutral or dark hijab colors are often preferred for formal settings to maintain professionalism. Minimal accessories and neat styling ensure the overall look stays clean and sophisticated.
