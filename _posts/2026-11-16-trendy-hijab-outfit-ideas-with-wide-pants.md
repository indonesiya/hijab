Pairing wide pants with fitted tops creates balance. Avoid overly loose combinations. Structured elements improve appearance. A balanced outfit feels more polished. Comfort remains important.

Accessories should match the outfit style. Simple bags and shoes work best. Avoid excessive styling. Keep the look clean. Small details enhance the outfit.
