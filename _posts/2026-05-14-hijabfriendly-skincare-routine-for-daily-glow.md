Maintaining healthy and glowing skin is important for every woman, including those who wear hijab. Since parts of the skin are often covered, it is essential to adopt a skincare routine that keeps the skin fresh, clean, and well-nourished throughout the day.

Cleansing your face regularly helps remove dirt, oil, and impurities that can build up over time. Using a gentle cleanser suited to your skin type ensures that your skin remains balanced without becoming too dry or oily.

Moisturizing is a crucial step that should never be skipped. A good moisturizer helps maintain hydration and protects the skin barrier, especially in different weather conditions.

Sunscreen is equally important, even for hijabi women. The face is still exposed to sunlight, and protecting it from harmful UV rays can prevent premature aging and skin damage.
