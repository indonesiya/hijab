In today’s digital age, managing screen time and online activities is essential for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. For hijabi women, creating a balanced digital lifestyle helps improve focus, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being.

One important step is setting boundaries for screen usage. Limiting time on social media allows you to focus on more meaningful activities.

Organizing your digital space can also improve efficiency. Keeping files, apps, and information structured reduces mental clutter.

Taking regular breaks from digital devices is equally important. Disconnecting for short periods helps refresh your mind and maintain balance.
