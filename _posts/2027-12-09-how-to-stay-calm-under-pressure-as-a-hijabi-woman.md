Staying calm under pressure is a valuable skill that can improve both personal and professional life. For hijabi women managing multiple responsibilities, the ability to remain composed during stressful situations is essential for maintaining balance and clarity.

One effective way to stay calm is by controlling your breathing. Deep and steady breaths can help reduce stress and allow you to think more clearly in challenging moments.

Preparing in advance can also reduce pressure. When you are well-prepared, you feel more confident and capable of handling unexpected situations.

Practicing mindfulness is another helpful technique. Staying present in the moment prevents overthinking and helps you focus on what needs to be done.
