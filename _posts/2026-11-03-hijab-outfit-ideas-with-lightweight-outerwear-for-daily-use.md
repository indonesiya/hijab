Lightweight outerwear is an essential part of hijab fashion, especially for layering in different weather conditions. It adds dimension to your outfit without making it feel heavy.

Pieces such as kimonos, light jackets, and thin cardigans are perfect for daily wear. They provide coverage while maintaining comfort.

Choosing breathable fabrics ensures that your outfit remains suitable for warm climates.

Neutral tones make outerwear easy to combine with various outfits, increasing its versatility.
