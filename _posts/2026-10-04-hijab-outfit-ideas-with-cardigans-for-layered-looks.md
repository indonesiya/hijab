Cardigans are one of the most versatile layering pieces in hijab fashion. They provide additional coverage while adding texture and depth to your outfit.

Long cardigans are particularly popular because they create a flowing silhouette that enhances modesty. They can be paired with dresses, skirts, or pants.

Choosing lightweight materials ensures comfort, especially when layering multiple pieces. This prevents the outfit from feeling heavy or restrictive.

Color coordination is important when styling cardigans. Neutral tones are easy to mix and match, while bold colors can add interest to your look.
