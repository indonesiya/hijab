Rainy and cold weather require hijab outfits that are not only stylish but also practical and comfortable. Layering becomes essential, with long coats, cardigans, and sweaters helping to keep the body warm while maintaining modest fashion.

Choosing thicker hijab materials like pashmina or wool blends helps protect against cold wind and moisture. Waterproof shoes and darker outfit colors are often preferred to avoid visible stains during rainy days.
