Managing time effectively is essential for anyone with a busy schedule, and for hijabi women balancing multiple roles, it becomes even more important. Developing strong time management skills can lead to a more organized and less stressful life.

One of the most effective strategies is setting clear priorities. Identifying what matters most helps you allocate your time and energy efficiently, ensuring that important tasks are completed first.

Creating a daily schedule can also improve productivity. By planning your day in advance, you reduce uncertainty and make it easier to stay focused on your responsibilities.

Avoiding procrastination is another key factor. Breaking tasks into smaller steps can make them feel more manageable, encouraging you to take action without feeling overwhelmed.
