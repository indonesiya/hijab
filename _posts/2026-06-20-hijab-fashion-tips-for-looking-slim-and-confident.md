Many hijabi women look for fashion tips that help create a slimmer and more balanced appearance. Choosing vertical lines in outfits, such as long cardigans or straight-cut dresses, helps elongate the body shape.

Darker colors are often used to create a more slimming and elegant visual effect. Avoiding overly bulky layers can help maintain a clean and structured silhouette.
