Keeping your hijab fresh throughout the day can be challenging, especially in warm weather or during busy activities. However, a few simple habits can help maintain a clean and comfortable feel.

Choosing breathable fabrics is one of the most important factors. Materials that allow airflow can reduce sweat and keep you feeling fresh longer.

Washing your hijabs regularly and using mild detergent helps maintain their cleanliness and softness. Proper care is essential for long-term use.

Using a light fragrance or fabric spray can add an extra touch of freshness without being overwhelming. Small details enhance your experience.
