Confidence in wearing the hijab often becomes stronger and more stable as time passes. What starts as a learning process gradually turns into a natural sense of self-assurance.

In the early stages, uncertainty and self-awareness are common. Adjusting to new habits and environments requires time and patience.

With consistent practice, these feelings begin to fade. Familiarity replaces doubt, and confidence starts to build naturally.

Experiences, both positive and challenging, contribute to this stability. Each situation strengthens resilience and self-belief.
