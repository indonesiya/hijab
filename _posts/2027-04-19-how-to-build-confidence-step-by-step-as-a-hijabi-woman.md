Confidence is not built overnight. For many hijabi women, it grows gradually through experiences, challenges, and self-acceptance.

The first step is accepting yourself as you are. This creates a strong foundation for personal growth and emotional stability.

Next is learning to ignore unnecessary comparisons. Everyone has a different journey, and progress should be measured individually.

Surrounding yourself with positive influences can also strengthen your confidence and motivation.
