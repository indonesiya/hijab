Choosing the right hijab style is not just about trends, but also about understanding your face shape. Every face is unique, and the way you style your hijab can enhance your natural features beautifully.

For round faces, creating volume at the top can help elongate the appearance of your face. Avoid wrapping the hijab too tightly around your cheeks, as this can make your face look fuller.

If you have an oval face shape, you are in luck because most styles will suit you well. You can experiment with different wraps and layers to find the look that best expresses your personality.

Square face shapes benefit from soft, flowing fabrics that help soften sharp angles. Draping the hijab loosely around the jawline can create a more balanced look.
