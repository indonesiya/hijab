Overshirts have become a trendy layering piece in casual hijab fashion. Their relaxed fit makes them comfortable for everyday wear. They can be worn open or buttoned depending on the desired style. This flexibility makes overshirts highly versatile. Many women use them to create effortless layered looks.

Pairing overshirts with basic inner outfits keeps the look balanced. Plain t-shirts or long-sleeve tops work well as a base. This allows the overshirt to stand out as the main layer. Neutral or earthy tones are commonly chosen for a modern aesthetic. These colors are easy to combine with other pieces.

Choosing the right fabric enhances comfort and style. Lightweight materials are ideal for daily use. They allow movement without feeling heavy. Adding simple accessories can complete the outfit. However, keeping things minimal helps maintain the casual vibe.
