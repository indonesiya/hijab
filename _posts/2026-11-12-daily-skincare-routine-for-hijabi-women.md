Wearing a hijab daily means that your skin, especially around the face and neck area, needs extra attention. A proper skincare routine can help maintain healthy and glowing skin despite environmental factors.

Cleansing your face twice a day is essential to remove dirt, oil, and makeup residue. This step ensures that your skin remains fresh and free from breakouts.

Hydration is equally important, as wearing a hijab for long hours can sometimes cause the skin to feel dry. Using a good moisturizer helps keep your skin soft and nourished.

Sunscreen should never be skipped, even if you feel protected by your hijab. UV rays can still affect exposed areas of your face, leading to premature aging.
