Managing time effectively is essential for hijabi women balancing work, family, and personal responsibilities. With so many tasks to handle, having a structured approach can make daily life more manageable and less stressful.

Prioritizing tasks is one of the most important steps in time management. Identifying what needs immediate attention and what can be postponed helps create clarity and focus.

Creating a daily schedule or to-do list allows better organization. Breaking tasks into smaller steps makes them easier to complete and reduces overwhelm.

Avoiding distractions, especially from digital devices, can significantly improve productivity. Setting boundaries for screen time helps maintain focus on important tasks.
