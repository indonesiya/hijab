Fashion mistakes are a natural part of learning and developing personal style. For hijabi women, understanding common mistakes can help improve overall appearance and confidence.

One common mistake is over-layering, which can make outfits look bulky and uncomfortable. Keeping layers balanced is important.

Another issue is poor color coordination, which can make even expensive outfits look less appealing. Harmony in colors enhances style.

Wearing inappropriate fabrics for certain weather conditions can also affect comfort and appearance. Choosing the right material is essential.
