Busy schedules can make it difficult to plan outfits every day. For hijabi women, having a simple system can save time and reduce stress.

Preparing outfits the night before is one of the most effective strategies. It allows you to start your day smoothly.

Organizing your wardrobe makes it easier to find matching items quickly.

Keeping a few go-to combinations helps you avoid last-minute decisions.
