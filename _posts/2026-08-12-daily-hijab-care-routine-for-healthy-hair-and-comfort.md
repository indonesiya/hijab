Maintaining healthy hair while wearing a hijab daily can sometimes feel challenging, but with the right routine, it becomes much easier. A proper hair care routine not only keeps your hair strong and shiny but also ensures comfort throughout the day. Many hijab-wearing women often overlook simple habits that can make a big difference.

One essential step is to always make sure your hair is completely dry before wearing your hijab. Damp hair trapped under fabric can lead to scalp issues such as dandruff or unpleasant odors. Allowing your hair to air dry or using a blow dryer on a low setting can prevent these problems effectively.

Choosing the right inner cap is equally important. Opt for breathable materials like cotton that allow airflow and reduce sweat buildup. Avoid wearing tight caps for long periods, as they can cause tension and lead to hair breakage over time.

Regular washing and conditioning are also crucial for maintaining scalp health. Use gentle shampoos that suit your hair type and avoid excessive use of heavy products that may clog your scalp. Keeping your hair clean helps prevent itchiness and discomfort.
