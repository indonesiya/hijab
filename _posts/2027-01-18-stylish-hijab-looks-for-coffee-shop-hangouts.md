Spending time at a coffee shop has become a popular lifestyle activity, especially for young hijabi women who enjoy relaxing while staying productive. Choosing the right outfit can enhance both comfort and style during these moments.

A simple yet trendy look can be achieved by pairing a loose blouse with straight jeans and a neutral hijab. This combination creates a relaxed but still fashionable appearance.

Soft color tones such as beige, cream, or pastel shades work well in casual settings and create a warm and inviting look.

Comfortable footwear like sneakers or flats completes the outfit, making it suitable for long hours of sitting or light activities.
