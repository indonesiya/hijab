Comparison is one of the biggest challenges in today’s social media-driven world, especially in fashion and hijab style.

Everyone has a different journey, body type, and personal preference, which makes comparison unfair and unhelpful.

Focusing on your own progress helps build confidence and self-acceptance.

Social media often shows only highlights, not reality, so it should be viewed with awareness.
