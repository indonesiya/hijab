Layering is an essential technique in modest fashion that allows hijabi women to create stylish and functional outfits. It adds depth and dimension to a look while ensuring that clothing remains modest and appropriate for different occasions.

One effective layering method is combining a long inner dress with an outer piece such as a cardigan or kimono. This not only enhances the outfit visually but also provides flexibility in adjusting to different environments and temperatures.

Playing with textures can also make a layered outfit more interesting. Mixing fabrics such as cotton, chiffon, and knitwear creates contrast and adds a unique touch to your overall style without being overwhelming.

Proportion is an important factor when layering. Balancing loose and fitted pieces ensures that the outfit does not appear bulky. Paying attention to how each layer fits can help maintain a clean and polished look.
