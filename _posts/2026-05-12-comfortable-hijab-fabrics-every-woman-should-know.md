Choosing the right hijab fabric is essential for comfort, especially for women who wear hijab for long hours every day. Cotton hijabs are widely used because they are breathable, soft, and suitable for hot weather conditions.

Chiffon hijabs are also popular due to their lightweight texture and elegant drape, making them ideal for both casual and formal looks. Jersey hijabs are known for their stretchability and ease of styling, making them perfect for busy women who need practical fashion solutions.
