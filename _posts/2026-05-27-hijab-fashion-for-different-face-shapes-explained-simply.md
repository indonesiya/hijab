Choosing the right hijab style based on face shape can improve overall appearance and balance facial features. Round faces often suit layered hijab styles that add definition and create a slimmer look.

Oval faces are considered versatile and can wear almost any hijab style comfortably and stylishly. Square faces look better with softer draping styles that reduce sharp angles and add elegance.
