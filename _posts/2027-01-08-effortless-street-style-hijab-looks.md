Street style has become a major inspiration for modern fashion, including for hijabi women who want to look stylish in a relaxed and natural way. This style focuses on comfort while still maintaining a trendy and expressive appearance.

A popular street style look includes oversized hoodies paired with loose pants and a simple hijab. This combination creates a laid-back yet fashionable outfit that is perfect for daily activities.

Neutral tones or earthy colors are often used to create a cohesive and effortless vibe. These colors are easy to mix and match with different wardrobe pieces.

Sneakers and casual accessories can enhance the overall street style look without making it feel too formal. Comfort is always a priority in this style.
