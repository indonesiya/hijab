Button-up shirts are a versatile piece that can be styled in many ways for hijab outfits. They offer a clean and structured look that works well for both casual and semi-formal occasions.

Pairing a button-up shirt with loose pants or a long skirt creates a balanced and modest outfit.

You can wear the shirt tucked, untucked, or layered with outerwear to create different looks from one piece.

Neutral colors are ideal for versatility, while patterned shirts can add personality to your outfit.
