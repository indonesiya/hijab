Oversized shirts are a popular choice for casual hijab outfits. They provide comfort and a relaxed look. Loose silhouettes allow easy movement. Neutral tones improve versatility. This style is perfect for daily activities.

Pairing oversized shirts with fitted bottoms creates balance. Avoid overly loose combinations. Structured elements improve appearance. A balanced outfit feels more polished. Comfort remains essential.

Accessories should remain simple for casual outfits. Minimal items work best. Avoid excessive styling. A clean look appears more effortless. Small details enhance the outfit.
