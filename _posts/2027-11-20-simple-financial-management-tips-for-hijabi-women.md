Managing finances is an important life skill that contributes to independence and stability. For hijabi women, understanding basic financial management can help create a secure and well-planned future.

One of the first steps is tracking your expenses. Knowing where your money goes allows you to make better decisions and avoid unnecessary spending.

Creating a budget is another essential practice. Allocating funds for different needs ensures that you can manage your income effectively while saving for future goals.

Developing a habit of saving is equally important. Even small amounts saved consistently can grow over time and provide financial security.
