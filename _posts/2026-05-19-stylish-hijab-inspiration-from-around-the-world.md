Hijab fashion has grown globally, with women from different countries adding their own cultural touch to modest styling. In Middle Eastern countries, hijab styles often feature elegant draping and luxurious fabrics for formal and daily wear.

In Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and Malaysia, hijab fashion tends to be more colorful, creative, and trend-focused. Western modest fashion influencers often combine hijab with modern streetwear for a unique and contemporary look.
