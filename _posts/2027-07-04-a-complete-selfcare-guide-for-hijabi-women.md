Self-care is an essential aspect of maintaining overall well-being, especially for hijabi women who balance various responsibilities in daily life. A comprehensive routine should address both physical and emotional needs.

Hair care is particularly important, as covering the hair for extended periods can lead to dryness or damage. Proper maintenance, including regular washing and hydration, is necessary to ensure healthy hair.

Skincare is another vital component. Maintaining a consistent routine helps prevent common issues caused by heat and humidity, ensuring that the skin remains clean and refreshed.

In addition to physical care, spiritual well-being plays a significant role. Engaging in mindfulness practices, reflection, and prayer can provide inner peace and emotional balance.
