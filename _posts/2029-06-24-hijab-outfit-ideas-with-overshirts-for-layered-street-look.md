Overshirts have become a popular layering piece in modern hijab fashion, offering a relaxed and trendy street style. These versatile garments can be worn open or closed, creating different looks with the same piece. Pairing an overshirt with a simple top and pants creates a balanced and effortless outfit. Neutral tones like beige, gray, and olive are commonly used for versatility. With the right styling, overshirts can elevate everyday outfits.

Choosing the right fabric is important for comfort and style. Lightweight cotton overshirts are ideal for warm weather, while thicker materials work better in cooler conditions. The fit should be slightly loose to maintain the relaxed aesthetic. Balancing proportions ensures the outfit does not look too bulky. Small adjustments can improve the overall look.

Hijab styling should complement the casual and layered nature of overshirts. Simple wraps with clean lines create a neat and modern appearance. Lightweight fabrics enhance comfort and practicality. Coordinating colors between the hijab and outfit ensures a cohesive look. This approach keeps the style fresh and stylish.
