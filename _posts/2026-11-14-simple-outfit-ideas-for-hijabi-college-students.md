Being a college student while maintaining a modest style can sometimes feel overwhelming, especially when you have limited time and budget. However, dressing modestly does not mean sacrificing style or creativity in your daily outfits.

One of the easiest outfit ideas is pairing a long tunic with straight pants and a neutral hijab. This combination creates a clean and effortless look that works perfectly for classes or casual campus activities.

Layering is also a great option, especially when transitioning between different weather conditions. Adding a lightweight cardigan or blazer can instantly elevate your outfit while keeping it modest.

Choosing comfortable fabrics is essential, as long hours on campus require outfits that allow you to move freely. Breathable materials like cotton and linen are excellent choices for daily wear.
