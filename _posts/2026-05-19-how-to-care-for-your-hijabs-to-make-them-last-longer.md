Proper care is essential to maintain the quality and longevity of your hijabs. Many women invest in beautiful pieces but may not always follow the right care routine, which can lead to fading, stretching, or damage over time. Taking simple steps can make a significant difference.

Washing your hijabs gently using mild detergent helps preserve the fabric. Avoid using harsh chemicals that can weaken the fibers and reduce the lifespan of your hijabs. Hand washing is often recommended for delicate materials.

Drying your hijabs properly is equally important. Instead of using high heat, air drying helps maintain the shape and texture of the fabric. This method is especially beneficial for materials like chiffon and silk.

Storing your hijabs neatly can prevent wrinkles and damage. Using hangers or folding them carefully ensures they remain in good condition and are easy to access when needed.
