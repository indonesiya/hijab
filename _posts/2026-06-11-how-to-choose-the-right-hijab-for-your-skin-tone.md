Choosing the right hijab color based on skin tone can enhance natural beauty and overall appearance. Fair skin tones often suit soft pastels and cool shades like blue or lavender.

Medium skin tones look great with warm colors such as olive, mustard, and earthy tones. Darker skin tones are beautifully complemented by bold and vibrant colors like emerald or burgundy.
