Neutral hijab colors remain popular because they are timeless, versatile, and easy to style. Shades like beige, taupe, cream, and grey are widely used in modern hijab fashion.

These colors work well for both casual and formal outfits, making them highly practical. Neutral tones also help create a clean and aesthetic visual appearance.
