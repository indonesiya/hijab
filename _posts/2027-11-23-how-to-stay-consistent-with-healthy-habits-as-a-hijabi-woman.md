Building healthy habits is one thing, but staying consistent with them is often the real challenge. For hijabi women, maintaining consistency is key to achieving long-term health and well-being.

One effective strategy is starting small. Simple habits are easier to maintain and can gradually develop into more significant lifestyle changes over time.

Creating a routine can also support consistency. When healthy habits become part of your daily schedule, they require less effort to maintain.

Staying motivated is another important factor. Reminding yourself of the benefits of your habits can encourage you to stay committed even when it feels difficult.
