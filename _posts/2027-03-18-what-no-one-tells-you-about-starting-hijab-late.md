Starting to wear the hijab later in life can feel challenging, especially when you compare yourself to others who started earlier. However, every journey is unique and valid in its own way.

One of the biggest challenges is overcoming self-doubt. You may feel like you’re “late” or worry about how others will react. These feelings are normal but should not hold you back.

The truth is, starting later often comes with a deeper understanding and stronger intention. You are making a conscious choice based on your own awareness and growth.

Many women who start later find the experience more meaningful because they fully understand the significance behind their decision. This creates a stronger connection to their journey.
