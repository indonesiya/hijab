Layering is a powerful styling technique that allows hijabi women to create fashionable and modest outfits. It adds depth and dimension while maintaining full coverage.

One simple layering idea is combining a long inner dress with a cardigan or kimono. This creates a balanced and elegant silhouette.

Mixing different textures and fabrics can make your outfit more interesting and visually appealing. Variety enhances creativity.

Keeping your color palette consistent helps maintain harmony in layered outfits. Too many contrasting colors can make the look overwhelming.
