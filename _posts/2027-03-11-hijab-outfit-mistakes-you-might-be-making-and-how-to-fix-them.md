Styling a hijab can sometimes feel tricky, especially with so many trends and influences online. While experimenting is part of the process, there are common mistakes that can affect your overall look without you realizing it.

One common mistake is over-layering. While layering can add dimension, too many layers can make your outfit look bulky and unbalanced. Keeping it simple often creates a cleaner and more elegant appearance.

Another issue is mismatched colors. Choosing too many bold colors in one outfit can make it look overwhelming. Instead, try sticking to a consistent color palette for a more harmonious look.

Ignoring fabric quality is also a big factor. Low-quality materials can make even the best outfit look less appealing. Investing in better fabrics can instantly upgrade your style.
