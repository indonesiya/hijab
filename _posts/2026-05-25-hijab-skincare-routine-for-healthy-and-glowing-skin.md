Taking care of skin is very important for hijabi women because the skin is often covered for long hours every day. A good skincare routine starts with gentle cleansing to remove dirt, oil, and sweat that can build up under the hijab.

Moisturizing is essential to keep the skin hydrated and prevent dryness caused by heat or friction from fabrics. Sunscreen is still necessary even for hijabi women, especially when spending time outdoors during the day.
