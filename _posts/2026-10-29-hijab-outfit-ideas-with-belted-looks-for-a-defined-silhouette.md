Belted outfits are a great way to add structure and shape to modest fashion without compromising coverage. For hijab women, this styling technique can create a more defined and balanced silhouette.

Wearing a belt over a loose dress or tunic can help highlight your waist while still maintaining modesty. This adds dimension to your outfit.

Choosing the right belt is important. Slim belts create a subtle effect, while wider belts make a stronger fashion statement.

Coordinating the belt color with your outfit helps maintain a cohesive and polished look.
