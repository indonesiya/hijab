Taking care of hijabs properly keeps them looking beautiful longer. Delicate scarves need gentle washing methods whenever possible. Hand washing is often the safest option for premium fabrics. Mild soap helps preserve texture and color. Careful washing prevents unnecessary damage.

Drying hijabs correctly is also very important. Heavy sunlight may fade darker colors over time. Air drying indoors can be a safer method. Laying scarves flat helps prevent stretching. Proper drying maintains the original shape.

Storage habits can also extend fabric life. Folding scarves neatly keeps them organized and easy to find. Hanging certain fabrics may reduce wrinkles. Separate light and dark colors for convenience. Organized storage saves time every day.
