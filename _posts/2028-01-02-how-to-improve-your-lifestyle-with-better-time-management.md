Time management is a key factor in creating a productive and balanced lifestyle. For hijabi women, managing time effectively helps balance responsibilities, personal growth, and well-being.

One important step is setting clear priorities. Knowing what needs to be done first helps you use your time efficiently.

Creating a daily schedule can also improve time management. Planning your day reduces confusion and increases productivity.

Avoiding procrastination is equally important. Breaking tasks into smaller steps can make them easier to complete.
