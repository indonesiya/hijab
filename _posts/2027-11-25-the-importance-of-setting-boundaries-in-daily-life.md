Setting boundaries is essential for maintaining a healthy and balanced lifestyle. For hijabi women, boundaries help protect time, energy, and emotional well-being while managing various responsibilities.

One important step in setting boundaries is understanding your limits. Recognizing what you can and cannot handle allows you to make better decisions and avoid unnecessary stress.

Communicating your boundaries clearly is also crucial. Being honest and respectful when expressing your needs helps others understand and respect your space.

Boundaries also apply to your time and commitments. Learning to say no when necessary ensures that you can focus on your priorities without feeling overwhelmed.
