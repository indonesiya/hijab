Wearing hijab is often misunderstood as something influenced by social pressure, but for many women, it is actually a deeply personal journey.

Each woman has her own reason and timing for starting the hijab, which makes every journey unique and meaningful.

Social expectations may exist, but true commitment comes from personal understanding and inner readiness.

Over time, the hijab becomes less about outside opinions and more about personal growth and values.
