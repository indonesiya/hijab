A capsule wardrobe is a smart way to simplify your hijab fashion while still looking stylish every day.

It focuses on essential pieces that can be mixed and matched easily.

Neutral colors and timeless designs make outfit combinations effortless.

This approach reduces clutter and saves time when choosing outfits.
