Self-care is not just a trend, it’s a necessity—especially for hijabi girls. Taking care of yourself physically and mentally should always be a priority.

Hair care is super important since it’s covered most of the time. Make sure you let it breathe and use the right products to keep it healthy.

Don’t forget skincare too. Wearing a hijab all day can make your skin feel warm, so keeping it clean and hydrated is key.

Mental and spiritual care matter just as much. Taking time to relax, reflect, or pray can really reset your mood and energy.
