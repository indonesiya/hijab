Hoodies are a versatile and comfortable piece that can easily be incorporated into hijab fashion. They are perfect for creating casual and street-style outfits that feel relaxed yet trendy.

Pairing a hoodie with loose pants or a long skirt creates a modest and stylish combination that works well for daily activities.

Choosing neutral or muted colors can help maintain a clean and cohesive look, while brighter colors add a playful touch.

Sneakers are the ideal footwear for hoodie outfits, as they enhance the casual and sporty vibe.
