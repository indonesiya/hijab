Layering is one of the most effective ways to create stylish and modest outfits. It allows you to combine different pieces while maintaining coverage and adding depth to your look.

A basic layering technique involves wearing a long inner top with an outer layer such as a cardigan or kimono. This combination creates a balanced and fashionable appearance.

Mixing textures, such as pairing soft fabrics with structured pieces, can make your outfit more visually interesting. It also adds a sense of creativity to your style.

Color coordination is important when layering, as too many contrasting colors can make the outfit look overwhelming. Sticking to a consistent palette helps maintain harmony.
