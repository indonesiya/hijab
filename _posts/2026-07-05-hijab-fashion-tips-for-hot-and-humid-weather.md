Hot and humid weather requires hijab styles that prioritize comfort and breathability. Lightweight fabrics like cotton and chiffon help reduce heat and keep the body cool.

Loose-fitting clothing allows better air circulation and improves comfort during the day. Simple hijab styles without heavy layers help prevent discomfort and overheating.
