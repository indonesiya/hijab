Distractions are everywhere, making it difficult to stay focused on important tasks. For hijabi women managing various responsibilities, improving focus is essential for productivity and success.

One way to avoid distractions is by creating a dedicated workspace. A quiet and organized area helps you concentrate better on your tasks.

Setting clear goals for each day can also improve focus. Knowing what needs to be accomplished keeps you on track.

Limiting unnecessary screen time is another effective strategy. Reducing distractions from devices helps maintain attention.
