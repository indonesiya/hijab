Many beginners in hijab fashion often struggle with small mistakes that affect comfort and appearance. One common mistake is using fabrics that are too slippery, making hijab difficult to style properly.

Another issue is choosing colors that do not match the outfit, creating an unbalanced look. Improper pin placement can also make hijab uncomfortable or look messy throughout the day.
