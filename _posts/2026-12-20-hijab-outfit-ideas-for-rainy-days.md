Dressing during rainy days can be challenging, especially when you want to stay dry while still looking stylish. Hijabi women can adapt their outfits to suit the weather without compromising comfort or modesty.

Choosing water-resistant outerwear such as a light jacket or coat can help protect your outfit from rain. This practical choice keeps you comfortable throughout the day.

Darker colors are often preferred during rainy weather because they are less likely to show stains or water marks. This helps maintain a neat appearance.

Wearing shoes that are suitable for wet conditions is also important. Comfortable and durable footwear can prevent discomfort during outdoor activities.
