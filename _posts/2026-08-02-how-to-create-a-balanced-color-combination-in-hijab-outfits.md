Color combination is one of the most important aspects of creating a stylish outfit. For hijabi women, understanding how to balance colors can enhance the overall appearance and create a more harmonious look.

Starting with a neutral base is a simple way to achieve balance. Neutral colors provide a foundation that can be paired with various shades without clashing.

Adding one or two accent colors can make your outfit more interesting without overwhelming it. This creates a focal point while maintaining harmony.

Avoiding too many bold colors in one outfit helps keep the look clean and organized. Simplicity often results in a more elegant appearance.
