Long outerwear adds elegance to hijab outfits. Pieces like cardigans and coats create a refined look. They are suitable for various occasions. Neutral colors enhance versatility. Outerwear can transform simple outfits.

Layering with outerwear requires balance. Avoid overly bulky combinations. Lightweight inner layers improve comfort. Proper fit enhances appearance. A structured look feels more polished.

Choosing the right length is important. Longer pieces create a taller silhouette. This improves overall proportions. Balance between top and bottom is essential. A well-fitted outfit looks more elegant.
