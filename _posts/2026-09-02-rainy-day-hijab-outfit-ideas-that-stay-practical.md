Dressing during rainy days can be challenging, especially when you want to maintain both comfort and style as a hijab woman. The key is choosing outfits that are practical while still looking neat and well put together.

Opting for darker colors can help prevent visible stains from rain or mud. Shades like black, navy, and dark brown are not only practical but also easy to style for various occasions.

Waterproof or quick-drying fabrics are highly recommended during rainy weather. These materials help you stay comfortable and avoid the inconvenience of damp clothing throughout the day.

Footwear is another important factor. Choosing closed shoes or water-resistant footwear can protect your feet and keep your overall look tidy.
