Dressing modestly during summer can be challenging due to high temperatures. Choosing the right fabrics is essential to stay comfortable throughout the day. Lightweight materials such as cotton, linen, and chiffon allow better airflow. These fabrics help reduce heat and keep the body cool. Comfort becomes the foundation of summer fashion.

Loose-fitting outfits are ideal for warm weather. Flowy dresses, wide-leg pants, and long skirts provide both coverage and ventilation. Light colors such as white, pastel, and beige reflect sunlight and enhance comfort. These choices create a fresh and breezy appearance. They are perfect for outdoor activities and daily wear.

Simplifying hijab styles can also improve comfort during summer. Styles with fewer layers and pins reduce heat and pressure. Breathable underscarves help maintain a neat look without discomfort. Minimal accessories are recommended to avoid feeling heavy. This ensures a practical and stylish outfit.
