Layering can enhance your outfit, but it needs to be done carefully to avoid looking bulky. For hijabi women, smart layering techniques can create a clean and polished appearance.

Using lightweight layers instead of heavy ones helps maintain comfort while still adding depth to your outfit. Balance is important in layering.

Keeping your layers in similar color tones creates a harmonious look that feels more organized and elegant.

Avoiding excessive layering ensures that your outfit remains practical and easy to wear throughout the day.
