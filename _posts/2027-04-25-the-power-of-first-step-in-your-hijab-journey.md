Every meaningful journey starts with a first step, and the hijab journey is no different. That first step often requires courage and self-belief.

Many women delay starting because they wait for perfect conditions, but progress begins with action, not perfection.

Taking the first step helps build confidence and clarity over time.

Each small effort leads to greater understanding and stronger commitment.
