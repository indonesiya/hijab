Layering is a popular technique in hijab fashion that allows women to create stylish and modest outfits. When done correctly, layering can add depth, versatility, and elegance to your overall look.

Choosing lightweight layers is essential to avoid feeling heavy or uncomfortable. Fabrics that are breathable and easy to move in help maintain comfort throughout the day.

Combining different lengths, such as long inner pieces with shorter outerwear, can create a visually interesting and balanced silhouette. This adds dimension without making the outfit look crowded.

Color coordination is also important when layering. Sticking to a cohesive color palette helps maintain a clean and organized appearance.
