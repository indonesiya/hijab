Layered textures add depth and uniqueness to hijab outfits. Combining different fabrics creates visual interest. Soft and structured materials work well together. Neutral tones keep the look balanced. This style feels modern and creative.

Proper layering requires attention to detail. Avoid overly bulky combinations. Lightweight fabrics improve comfort. Balance between layers is essential. A structured look feels more polished.

Accessories should support the layered style. Keep them simple to avoid clutter. Neutral items work best. This maintains consistency. Small details enhance the outfit.
