Beginning anything new can feel challenging, and wearing the hijab is no exception. The initial phase often comes with discomfort, self-doubt, and adjustment.

This difficulty is a natural part of change. You are stepping out of your comfort zone and adapting to a new way of life.

As you continue, the unfamiliar becomes familiar. What once felt difficult starts to feel normal and comfortable.

Support, routine, and self-belief all play important roles in making the process easier over time.
