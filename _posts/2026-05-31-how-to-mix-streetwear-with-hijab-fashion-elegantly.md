Streetwear hijab fashion combines modern urban clothing with modest wear, creating a bold yet stylish appearance. Oversized hoodies, wide-leg pants, and sneakers are often used as the foundation of streetwear-inspired hijab outfits.

Neutral or monochrome hijabs help balance the boldness of streetwear clothing for a more elegant finish. Accessories like caps or crossbody bags can enhance the urban aesthetic without compromising modesty.
