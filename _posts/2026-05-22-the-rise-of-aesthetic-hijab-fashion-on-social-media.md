Social media has played a major role in shaping modern hijab fashion trends by introducing aesthetic and visually appealing styles. Platforms like Instagram and TikTok are filled with hijab outfit inspirations that encourage creativity and self-expression.

Aesthetic hijab fashion often focuses on soft tones, minimal backgrounds, and clean outfit combinations. Influencers help spread global trends, making it easier for women to discover new hijab styles from different cultures.
