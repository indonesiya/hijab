Taking proper care of your hijabs is essential to keep them looking fresh and long-lasting. Many women overlook this aspect, but good maintenance can significantly extend the lifespan of your favorite pieces.

Washing your hijabs with gentle detergent helps preserve the fabric and color. It is important to avoid harsh chemicals that may damage delicate materials such as chiffon or silk.

Drying your hijabs naturally is also recommended, as excessive heat from dryers can weaken the fibers. Air drying helps maintain the softness and shape of the fabric.

Proper storage is another key factor. Folding or hanging your hijabs neatly prevents wrinkles and makes them easier to access when needed.
