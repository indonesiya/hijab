Stress is a common part of modern life, and managing it effectively is essential for maintaining well-being. Hijabi women often juggle multiple responsibilities, making stress management even more important.

Identifying the sources of stress is the first step in addressing it. Understanding what triggers stress allows you to find appropriate solutions.

Practicing relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or taking short breaks can help reduce tension and improve focus.

Maintaining a balanced routine that includes rest and self-care is crucial for long-term stress management.
