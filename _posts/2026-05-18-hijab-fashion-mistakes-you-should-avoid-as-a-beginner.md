Many beginners in hijab fashion often make simple mistakes that affect comfort and overall appearance. One common mistake is choosing the wrong fabric that is either too slippery or too heavy, making it difficult to style properly.

Another mistake is over-accessorizing, which can make the hijab look too crowded instead of neat and balanced. Ignoring face shape when choosing hijab style can also affect how the overall outfit looks and feels.
