Understanding your face shape can help you choose hijab styles that enhance your natural features and create a more balanced look. Many women experiment with different styles without realizing that certain techniques work better for specific face shapes.

For round faces, creating volume at the top of the head can help elongate the appearance. Avoid wrapping the hijab too tightly around the cheeks, as it can make the face look wider.

Oval face shapes are considered the most versatile, allowing for a wide range of styles. You can experiment with different draping techniques and still achieve a balanced look.

Square faces benefit from softer, more rounded hijab styles that reduce the appearance of sharp angles. Loose draping around the jawline can create a more gentle and feminine look.
