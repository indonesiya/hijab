Stress is a natural part of life, but managing it effectively is crucial for maintaining both mental and physical health. For hijabi women handling multiple responsibilities, developing stress management strategies can greatly improve daily well-being.

One effective approach is identifying the sources of stress. Understanding what triggers your stress allows you to take proactive steps to address or minimize those factors.

Practicing relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or quiet reflection can help calm the mind and reduce tension during stressful moments.

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle also plays a role in stress management. Regular exercise, proper nutrition, and sufficient rest all contribute to better emotional balance.
