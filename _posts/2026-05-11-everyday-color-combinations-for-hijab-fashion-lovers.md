Color combination plays a very important role in hijab fashion because it determines the overall harmony and visual appeal of an outfit. Many hijab fashion lovers prefer neutral tones like beige, white, and grey because they are easy to match with almost any clothing style.

Earth tones such as brown, olive, and mocha are also popular because they create a warm and natural aesthetic suitable for daily wear. For a more bold look, some women experiment with contrasting colors, such as pairing dark hijabs with light outfits or vice versa.
