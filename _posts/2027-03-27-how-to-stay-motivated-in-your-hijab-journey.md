Staying consistent in wearing the hijab can sometimes be challenging, especially during difficult days. Motivation may come and go, but commitment is what keeps you going.

One way to stay motivated is by reminding yourself why you started. Keeping your intentions clear helps you stay focused during moments of doubt.

Surrounding yourself with supportive people can also boost your motivation. Positive influences can encourage you to stay strong in your journey.

Consuming inspiring content, such as stories or advice from other hijabi women, can provide fresh motivation and perspective.
