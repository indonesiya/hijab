Pleated tops add a unique texture to hijab outfits, creating visual interest without requiring bold patterns. The structured folds create depth and movement, making the outfit more dynamic. Pairing pleated tops with simple bottoms helps maintain balance. Neutral or soft tones enhance the elegance of the texture. With the right styling, pleated tops can create a sophisticated and modern look.

Choosing the right pleat size and fabric affects the overall appearance. Fine pleats create a subtle and refined look, while larger pleats make a stronger statement. Lightweight materials allow the pleats to move naturally and look more elegant. Balancing the top with structured pieces ensures a cohesive outfit. Small details can make a significant difference.

Hijab styling should remain simple to complement the textured top. Clean and minimal wraps help maintain focus on the pleats. Coordinating colors ensures a harmonious appearance. Avoid overly complex styles that may clash with the texture. This approach keeps the outfit polished and refined.
