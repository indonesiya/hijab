Starting to wear a hijab is a deeply personal decision, and the experience can be different for everyone. However, there are some common changes that many women notice once they begin this journey. Some are expected, while others might surprise you.

One of the first things you may feel is self-awareness. You become more conscious of your actions, your words, and how you present yourself. This awareness often leads to positive personal growth.

You might also notice changes in your social circle. Some people will become more supportive, while others may distance themselves. This process helps you identify who truly respects and values you.

Emotionally, there can be ups and downs. There will be days of confidence and days of doubt. But over time, the sense of peace and purpose becomes stronger than any temporary uncertainty.
