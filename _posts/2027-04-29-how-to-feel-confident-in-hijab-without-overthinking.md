Overthinking appearance can reduce confidence, especially when trying to look perfect every day in hijab.

The key is to simplify your routine and focus on what makes you feel comfortable.

Confidence grows when you stop comparing yourself to unrealistic standards.

Practicing self-acceptance helps you feel more natural and relaxed in your style.
