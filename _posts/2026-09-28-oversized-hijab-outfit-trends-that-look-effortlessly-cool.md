Oversized fashion has become a major trend in recent years, and it fits perfectly with modest hijab styling. This trend focuses on comfort, relaxed silhouettes, and a modern aesthetic that feels effortless.

Key pieces include oversized shirts, long outerwear, and wide-leg pants. These items create a loose and comfortable fit while maintaining a stylish appearance.

Balancing proportions is important when wearing oversized outfits. Pairing loose pieces with slightly structured elements can prevent the look from appearing too bulky.

Neutral tones and simple designs help maintain a clean and cohesive outfit. Minimal accessories work best to keep the focus on the overall silhouette.
