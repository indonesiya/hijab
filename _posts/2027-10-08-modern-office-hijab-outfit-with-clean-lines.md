Fabric selection plays a key role in comfort during long hours. Breathable materials are recommended for daily wear. Avoid overly thick fabrics. Lightweight options improve flexibility. Comfort supports productivity.

Accessories should be simple and professional. Minimal jewelry works best for office settings. Avoid flashy elements. A clean look reflects professionalism. Subtle details enhance elegance.
