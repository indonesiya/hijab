Maintaining a neat hijab throughout the day can be challenging, especially during long hours of activity. However, with the right techniques and preparation, you can keep your hijab looking fresh and well-arranged from morning to evening.

Choosing the right fabric is the first step. Materials that are easy to manage and less prone to slipping can help maintain your style without constant adjustments.

Securing your hijab properly with pins or clips ensures that it stays in place. It is important to find a balance between security and comfort to avoid discomfort during the day.

Avoiding excessive movement or touching your hijab can also help maintain its neatness. Small habits can make a significant difference in preserving your overall look.
