Having a large collection of hijabs can sometimes make it difficult to choose the right one quickly. Organizing your hijabs efficiently can save time and reduce stress in your daily routine.

One practical method is to sort your hijabs by color. This makes it easier to find matching pieces for your outfits without spending too much time searching.

You can also organize them based on material, such as chiffon, cotton, or jersey. This helps you select the most suitable option depending on the weather or occasion.

Using storage tools like hangers, boxes, or drawer dividers can keep your collection neat and accessible. A tidy space improves efficiency.
