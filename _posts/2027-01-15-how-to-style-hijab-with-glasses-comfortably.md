Wearing glasses with a hijab can sometimes be challenging, especially when it comes to comfort and fit. However, with the right techniques, you can achieve both style and practicality.

Choosing lightweight hijab fabrics helps reduce pressure around the ears. This makes it more comfortable to wear glasses for long periods.

Adjusting the way you wrap your hijab around the temples can prevent discomfort and ensure a better fit. Small adjustments make a big difference.

Opting for simple styles with minimal layers can also improve comfort while maintaining a neat appearance.
