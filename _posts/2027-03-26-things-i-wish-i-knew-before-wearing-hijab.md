Starting the hijab journey can come with many surprises. Looking back, there are several things many women wish they had known earlier to make the transition smoother.

One important realization is that it’s okay to feel unsure at first. Adjusting to a new lifestyle takes time, and it’s part of the process.

Another lesson is that not everyone will understand your decision. Learning to stay confident despite differing opinions is essential.

You also learn that style evolves over time. There’s no need to rush into finding the “perfect look” right away.
