While styling a hijab may seem simple, certain mistakes can affect your overall appearance. Being aware of these common issues can help you improve your style and confidence.

One mistake is choosing the wrong fabric for the occasion. Heavy materials in hot weather can cause discomfort and affect how your hijab sits.

Another common issue is poor color coordination, which can make your outfit look unbalanced. Matching colors properly enhances your overall style.

Wearing a hijab that is too tight or too loose can also impact both comfort and appearance. Finding the right balance is important.
