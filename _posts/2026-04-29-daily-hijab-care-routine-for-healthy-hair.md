Maintaining healthy hair while wearing a hijab is an important aspect that many women often overlook. Even though the hair is covered most of the time, it still requires proper care and attention to stay strong, shiny, and healthy. A consistent hair care routine can help prevent common issues such as dryness, breakage, and hair fall.

One essential step is ensuring your hair is completely dry before putting on your hijab. Wearing a hijab over damp hair can lead to scalp problems and unpleasant odors. Allowing your hair to breathe before covering it helps maintain a healthy scalp environment and reduces the risk of damage.

Choosing the right inner cap is also crucial. Opt for breathable materials like cotton to reduce friction and prevent excessive sweating. A comfortable inner cap can protect your hairline and keep your hijab in place throughout the day without causing discomfort.

Regular washing and conditioning are key to keeping your hair nourished. Using products that suit your hair type ensures that your hair receives the nutrients it needs. Incorporating natural oils can also add moisture and improve overall hair health.
