Layering is an essential technique in modest fashion, but it can sometimes make outfits look bulky if not done correctly. For hijab women, mastering the art of layering can help create stylish, modest looks that remain comfortable and flattering.

The first step is choosing lightweight fabrics that do not add unnecessary volume. Materials such as chiffon, linen, and soft cotton are excellent choices for layering because they provide coverage without heaviness.

Color coordination also plays a key role in creating a streamlined appearance. Sticking to similar tones or monochromatic outfits can make your layers look more cohesive and less overwhelming.

Proper fit is another important factor. While modest fashion often includes loose clothing, combining oversized pieces with more structured items can create balance and prevent your outfit from looking shapeless.
