Oversized layers are a key element in trendy street hijab fashion. These styles provide comfort and a bold appearance. Neutral and dark tones enhance versatility. Loose fits allow easy movement. Street style reflects individuality.

Balancing oversized layers is important for structure. Pairing with fitted pieces improves the overall look. Avoid overly baggy combinations. A balanced outfit feels more refined. Comfort remains essential.

Accessories should match the streetwear vibe. Sneakers and simple bags are ideal choices. Avoid excessive styling. Keep the look cohesive. Small details enhance the outfit.
