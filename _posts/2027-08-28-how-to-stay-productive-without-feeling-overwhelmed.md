Productivity is important, but it should not come at the cost of mental well-being. For hijabi women, balancing productivity and relaxation is key to avoiding burnout.

Breaking tasks into smaller steps can make them more manageable. This approach reduces stress and makes it easier to stay focused.

Taking regular breaks helps maintain energy levels and prevents exhaustion. Short pauses can improve overall performance.

Setting realistic expectations is also important. Trying to do too much at once can lead to frustration and decreased motivation.
