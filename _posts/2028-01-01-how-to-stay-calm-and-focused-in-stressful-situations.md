Stressful situations are a part of life, but staying calm and focused can make a significant difference in how you handle them. For hijabi women, maintaining composure is essential for making clear decisions and managing challenges effectively.

One way to stay calm is by practicing deep breathing. This simple technique helps reduce tension and improves mental clarity.

Preparing yourself mentally can also help manage stress. Anticipating challenges allows you to respond more effectively when they arise.

Taking a step back during stressful moments can further improve focus. Pausing briefly helps you regain control and think more clearly.
