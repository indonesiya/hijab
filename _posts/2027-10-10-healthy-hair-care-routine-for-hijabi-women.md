Maintaining healthy hair while wearing a hijab daily requires special attention and care. Since hair is often covered for long hours, it is important to adopt a routine that ensures proper nourishment, cleanliness, and ventilation to prevent common issues such as dryness or hair fall.

One of the most important steps in hair care is keeping the scalp clean. Regular washing with a suitable shampoo helps remove sweat and buildup, which can accumulate more quickly due to prolonged coverage. Choosing gentle and hydrating products can make a noticeable difference.

Allowing your hair to breathe is equally essential. Whenever possible, spend time at home without wearing a hijab to let your scalp get fresh air. This simple habit can improve overall hair health and reduce discomfort.

Using the right inner hijab or underscarf also plays a significant role. Opting for breathable fabrics such as cotton can reduce friction and prevent excessive sweating, keeping both hair and scalp more comfortable throughout the day.
