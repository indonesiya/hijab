Soft color palettes have become increasingly popular among hijabi women who want to achieve a gentle and feminine appearance. These colors create a calm and elegant impression.

Shades like pastel pink, baby blue, and lavender are commonly used to enhance a soft and graceful look. These tones are easy to pair with neutral outfits.

Combining soft colors with flowy fabrics can further enhance the feminine aesthetic. The overall look becomes more delicate and refined.

Keeping makeup and accessories minimal helps maintain balance and prevents the outfit from looking too overwhelming.
