Fashion rules help hijabi women create balanced and stylish outfits without overcomplicating their look. One simple rule is balancing loose clothing with structured pieces to maintain a neat silhouette.

Another rule is choosing colors that complement each other instead of clashing. Comfort should always come first, especially for daily hijab wear and long activities.
