Maintaining healthy hair is essential for hijabi women, as hair is often covered for long periods throughout the day. Proper care ensures that the hair remains strong, hydrated, and free from damage.

One important tip is to allow the hair to breathe whenever possible. Spending time without the hijab at home helps reduce moisture buildup and prevents scalp issues.

Choosing the right hair products also makes a significant difference. Using shampoos and conditioners that suit your hair type can improve overall hair health and reduce breakage.

In addition, selecting breathable hijab fabrics can help maintain scalp comfort. Materials like cotton allow better air circulation compared to heavier fabrics.
