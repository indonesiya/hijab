Staying positive in a negative environment can be incredibly challenging, especially when external influences affect your mindset and emotions. For hijabi women, maintaining positivity is essential to protect mental well-being and stay aligned with personal values.

One effective strategy is controlling what you can influence. While you may not be able to change your surroundings completely, you can choose how you respond to them and protect your energy.

Surrounding yourself with positivity, even in small ways, can make a difference. Listening to uplifting content or connecting with supportive individuals can help shift your mindset.

Practicing self-awareness is also important. Recognizing when negativity starts affecting you allows you to step back and reset your thoughts.
