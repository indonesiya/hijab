Proper hijab care helps fabrics last longer and look fresh. Many delicate materials need gentle washing methods. Reading the care label is always a smart first step. Using mild detergent can protect soft fibers. Good care habits maintain color and texture.

Hand washing is often best for chiffon and silk fabrics. Rough machine cycles may damage delicate scarves. If machine washing is necessary, use a laundry bag. Cold water is usually safer than hot water. Gentle treatment preserves fabric quality.

Drying methods also matter for hijab maintenance. Hanging scarves flat can prevent stretching. Direct sunlight may fade some colors over time. Ironing on low heat helps remove wrinkles safely. Proper drying keeps hijabs looking elegant.
